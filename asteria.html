About Asteria — Culturals of Hope and Joy 🪅
www.asteria.org/about  —  Last updated: January 2026  —  Cached
★ Welcome to Asteria 2026 — our fourth annual celebration of student talent and college culture. June 2026, Madha Medical College & Research Institute.

Our Mission

Asteria is an annual cultural festival where medicos show their talents in Music, Dance, Arts, Poetry, Sports and other Extra Curricular Activities. Founded 2022. Now in its fourth year.

Every edition is curated around a single guiding question: "What does it mean to belong to a culture?" This year's theme is Roots & Routes — exploring how heritage travels, transforms, and survives across generations.

History

From Magizchi to Asteria.... from house wise sports events to batch wise sports events...... from 10+ overall competitive events to 50+ events.......

Now in its fourth year, Asteria has grown into the premier celebration of student talent at Madha Medical College and Research Institute, bringing together students from all batches in a spirit of friendly competition and creative expression.

Yaakai — The Cultural Heart of Asteria
www.asteria.org/yaakai  —  Programme updated: June 2026  —  Cached
Yaakai (யாக்கை) — meaning "body" or "vessel" in Tamil — is the living cultural programme at the core of Asteria, presenting traditional arts, world music, and film from South Asia and the Tamil diaspora.

About Yaakai

Yaakai is a curated strand within Asteria that centres the voices, stories, and art forms of Tamil culture alongside broader South Asian and diasporic traditions. It celebrates the living body of culture — music, dance, film, poetry, and oral storytelling — as a vessel for collective memory.

The 2026 Yaakai programme features over 20 events across 7 days, including world premieres, archival screenings, live performances, and panel discussions.
